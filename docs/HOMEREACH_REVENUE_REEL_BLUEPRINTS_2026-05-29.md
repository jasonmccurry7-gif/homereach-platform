# HomeReach Revenue Reel Blueprints

Date: 2026-05-29

## Strategic Decision

Higgsfield should not be treated as the creative director or final editor. The daily content system should treat Higgsfield and any future AI video provider as optional textless b-roll generation. HomeReach must control the revenue offer, English captions, CTA, proof, compliance notes, and final brand frame.

The reels should sell specific outcomes:

- Supplyfy: reduce supply overspending and protect margin.
- Targeted Mail: build route density around existing work.
- Shared Postcards: make homeowner visibility affordable.
- Political Mail: give campaigns clear mail options, costs, timing, and approval visibility.
- Government Contracts: help small businesses find and organize bid opportunities faster.
- AI Website Assistant: turn more website visitors into conversations.
- Local SEO and Visibility: make local buyers more likely to find the business.
- Reputation: protect trust and improve review follow-up.
- Property Intelligence: stop guessing where to market.
- Market Capture: connect local visibility, mail, follow-up, and execution.
- Yard Signs, Door Hangers, Business Cards: support the main growth engine with physical visibility and follow-up assets.

## Production Principle

Every reel must answer five things fast:

1. Who is this for?
2. What pain are they feeling?
3. What money, time, or visibility is being lost?
4. What does HomeReach or Supplyfy do?
5. What should the viewer comment or DM?

## Daily Active Reels

The system should keep two active daily generated reels:

- One Supplyfy reel focused on supply cost savings and margin protection.
- One HomeReach targeted mail reel focused on route density and neighborhood growth.

The full blueprint library supports expansion into other HomeReach offers without creating duplicate dashboards or fragile new content routes.

## AI Provider Guardrail

AI video providers must not render readable text. If the provider creates foreign text, pseudo-text, fake dashboards, fake documents, fake signage, distorted hands, or unclear service scenes, the asset should be rejected or used only as non-final b-roll.

All visible words should be added by the HomeReach overlay/editor layer:

- English only.
- Less than 8 words per scene.
- Mobile readable.
- Spelled correctly.
- Clear CTA keyword.

## Revenue Keywords

- SAVE: Supplyfy supply cost review.
- ROUTES: Targeted route density example.
- POSTCARD: Shared postcard spot availability.
- CAMPAIGN: Political mail options.
- BID: Government contract opportunity review.
- WEBSITE: AI website assistant review.
- LOCAL: Local visibility review.
- REVIEWS: Reputation check.
- MAP: Property targeting example.
- GROWTH: Market capture plan.
- SIGNS: Yard sign options.
- DOORS: Door hanger route plan.
- CARDS: Business card and brand kit.

## Approval Rules

These reels are designed to generate DMs and comments, not auto-publish. Human approval remains required before publishing, sending, posting, making political claims, using savings claims publicly, or attaching content to outreach.
