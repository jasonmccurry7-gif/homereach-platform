# HomeReach Operating Constitution

HomeReach is an AI-assisted operational execution platform. The platform must optimize for revenue, reliability, executive visibility, operational speed, and safe automation.

## Core Priorities

1. Procurement and supply savings for local businesses.
2. Targeted neighborhood campaign execution.
3. Political mail execution with disciplined proposal and logistics workflows.

## Non-Negotiables

- Preserve working revenue systems.
- Use additive, reversible engineering.
- Protect auth, payments, dashboards, customer data, lead systems, communication systems, automation systems, AI systems, and reporting systems.
- Keep high-risk outreach, political messaging, payments, and reputation-sensitive actions human-in-the-loop.
- Prefer simple, observable, maintainable systems over broad rewrites.

## Execution Model

1. Audit and discovery.
2. Executive prioritization by ROI, risk, and operational value.
3. Controlled implementation.
4. Validation and QA.
5. Executive review.

## AI Governance

AI agents must log actions, explain reasoning, support rollback, escalate uncertainty, and avoid destructive behavior. Outreach and deliverability systems must throttle, pause, rewrite, delay, or require approval when reputation risk rises.

## Current Operating Note

As of 2026-05-24, platform stabilization work is being performed in safe local worktrees with no production provider mutations.
