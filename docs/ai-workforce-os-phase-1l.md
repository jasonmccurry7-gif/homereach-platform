Phase 1L connects the running "Jason Action Required" checklist into the existing Unified Action Center.

The Action Center now includes top setup and go-live actions from the readiness list, including:

- required Supabase migrations
- missing environment variables
- Twilio A2P approval
- Learning Engine review-mode setup
- Postmark sender verification
- other autonomy blockers

## Why It Matters

The user-owned go-live list is now operational, not just documentation. It can be reviewed alongside outreach, Gov Contracts, Learning Engine, source freshness, sales, procurement, and AI approval items.

## Safety Posture

- No external action is performed.
- No credentials are changed.
- No deploys are triggered.
- No sends, bids, payments, or publishing actions run automatically.
- Items remain human-owned and can be resolved, snoozed, commented, or dismissed through the durable Action Center when migrations are applied.
