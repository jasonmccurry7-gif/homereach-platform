# Digital + Direct Mail Bundle SKU

Canonical managed bundle SKU:

```text
market_capture_digital_mail_founder
```

Use this SKU internally for any founder-rate Market Capture campaign that includes both:

- Market Capture digital management
- A direct mail add-on quoted separately after geography, quantity, proof, postage, and vendor cost are verified

## Client-Facing Offer

Digital + Direct Mail Campaign

- $499/month Market Capture management
- Client-funded ad spend
- Direct mail quoted separately
- Optional landing page or creative package quoted separately

## Founder-Rate Positioning

Approved wording:

```text
This founder-rate package lets you pair Market Capture digital visibility with postcards in the same target area. HomeReach manages the campaign plan, creative workflow, approval checklist, and reporting. Ad spend and direct mail production are separate so you stay in control of the budget.
```

Do not say:

- Direct mail is included in $499/month.
- Results are guaranteed.
- Every household saw both channels.
- Digital ads are automatically launched.
- Postcards are sent before proof and quote approval.

## Internal Package Rules

The $499/month management fee covers:

- Digital campaign planning
- Target area planning
- Creative guidance
- Manual launch checklist
- Tracking plan
- Monthly report preparation
- Direct mail add-on coordination

The direct mail quote covers:

- Mail quantity
- Print format
- Postage
- Vendor production
- Route or list preparation
- Any design or proofing outside included creative guidance

## Approval Gates

Before billing or production:

- Management subscription active or owner-approved invoice path.
- Client-funded ad spend confirmed.
- Mail geography verified.
- Quantity verified.
- Postcard proof approved.
- Vendor quote approved.
- Admin approval recorded.
