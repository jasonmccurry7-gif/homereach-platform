# AI Workforce OS Phase 4E: Action Center Policy Summary

## What changed

Phase 4E adds policy counts to the Unified Action Center summary.

New summary values:

- High Risk
- Internal Handoff Eligible

## Why it matters

Admins can now see how much of the operating queue requires tight human supervision and how much is eligible for safe internal handoff before opening the individual filters.

## Safety

This phase only adds summary visibility. It does not change permissions, trigger execution, send messages, place orders, alter payments, publish content, launch campaigns, submit bids, or deploy code.
