# AI Workforce OS Phase 4C: Guardrail-Aware Action Filters

## What changed

Phase 4C adds policy-aware filters to the Unified Action Center.

New filters:

- High Risk
- Internal Handoff

## Why it matters

Admins can now quickly separate actions that must stay tightly supervised from lower-risk items that are eligible for safe internal handoff. This helps HomeReach scale AI operations without blurring the line between recommendation and execution.

## Safety

The filters only change what is visible in the Action Center. They do not grant permissions, queue execution, send outreach, place orders, change payments, publish, launch campaigns, submit bids, or deploy code.
