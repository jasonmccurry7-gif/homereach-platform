## What It Means

Explain why the activity matters.

Use:

- Local visibility increased in the selected area.
- The offer is getting clicks but needs stronger conversion.
- The target area is too broad or too narrow.
- Direct mail should be added for repeated exposure.
- Creative should be refreshed.
- Tracking needs improvement before stronger conclusions are made.

Avoid:

- Guaranteed ROI.
- Guaranteed leads.
- "We tracked people" language.
- Attribution certainty when tracking is incomplete.

## Report QA Checklist

Before delivery, confirm:

- The report period is correct.
- Payment and campaign status are current.
- Ad spend is listed separately from the HomeReach management fee.
- All metrics are sourced or marked unavailable.
- CTR, CPC, and cost per lead are calculated only when inputs are available.
- Direct mail metrics are included only if the add-on was used.
- No guaranteed results, ROI, leads, visits, sales, rankings, or attribution certainty are claimed.
- Recommendations are specific and actionable.
- Internal notes are removed from the client-facing version.
- First real client report is owner-approved before sending.
