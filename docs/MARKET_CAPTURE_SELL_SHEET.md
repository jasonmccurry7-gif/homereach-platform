# Market Capture Sell Sheet

## Offer

Market Capture helps local businesses stay visible in the neighborhoods, jobsites, service areas, competitor areas, events, and high-value local geographies that matter most.

## Price

- Starter management fee: $499/month.
- Recommended starter ad spend: $1,000/month, client-funded separately.
- Recommended starter commitment: 3 months.
- Direct mail, landing pages, creative packages, political work, and larger geographies are quoted separately or sold as higher-scope packages.

## Client Value

The business owner gets a practical local visibility plan without needing to understand ad-tech complexity.

Market Capture is designed to help the client:

- Stay visible near their best customers.
- Turn jobsites into follow-up neighborhoods.
- Test local demand without a confusing agency retainer.
- Pair digital visibility with direct mail when repeated exposure matters.
- Track available performance metrics and next actions.

## What HomeReach Delivers

- Intake review.
- Target area planning.
- Campaign objective review.
- Ad spend and scope confirmation.
- Campaign plan.
- Basic creative guidance.
- Manual launch checklist.
- Optional direct mail discussion.
- Monthly report.
- Renewal or expansion recommendation.

## What Is Not Included By Default

- Ad spend.
- Guaranteed leads, sales, ROI, conversions, rankings, or visits.
- Automatic paid ad launch.
- Individual-level location identity display.
- Print/mail cost unless separately quoted.
- Landing page build unless added.
- Advanced creative package unless added.

## Sales Positioning

Use:

```text
HomeReach helps you stay visible in the local areas that already matter to your business: jobsites, neighborhoods, service areas, competitor areas, events, and high-value ZIPs. We build the plan, prepare the campaign workflow, help track the available results, and can pair the digital campaign with postcards to the same neighborhoods.
```

Avoid:

- "We track people."
- "We spy on competitors."
- "Guaranteed leads."
- "Guaranteed ROI."
- "We follow homeowners."
- "Ads launch automatically."

## Discovery Questions

- Which neighborhoods or service areas produce your best customers?
- Do you have recent jobsites we should build around?
- Are there competitor areas, events, or neighborhoods where you want more visibility?
- What monthly ad spend are you comfortable funding separately?
- Do you want postcards sent to the same target area?
- Do you have a strong offer, photos, logo, and a landing page?
- What would make this campaign successful enough to renew?

## Qualification Rules

Strong fit:

- Clear service area.
- Clear customer value.
- Enough margin to justify a paid campaign.
- Willing to fund ad spend separately.
- Understands results vary.

Poor fit:

- No target geography.
- No ad spend budget.
- Expects guaranteed leads or sales.
- Wants prohibited targeting.
- Wants ad spend included in the $499 management fee.
